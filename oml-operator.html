Operator – Manlifts

Company: OML Uganda Limited

Deadline: 24th June 2025

Expected Start Date: 1st July 2025

Description

OML Uganda Limited is hiring an Operator – Manlifts to be responsible for all operations associated with equipment that handles loads or provides lifting and hoisting services.

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Operate equipment in a safe and careful manner
  • Perform daily inspections on equipment
  • Identify errors, defects, or malfunctions
  • Log fuel usage, mileage, and gate pass/checklist entries
  • Report maintenance issues to site supervisor
  • Keep equipment clean and accessories stored properly
  • Ensure workplace health and safety
  • Proper handover of equipment on exit
  • Take over equipment responsibly on arrival
  • Perform other duties if equipment is not operational

Job Specification

Academic & Certificate Qualifications:
  • Valid driver/operator license for the equipment
  • Suitability test for specific equipment
  • Defensive driving certification
  • Minimum education level: Ordinary Level (S.4)
Minimum Experience Required:
  • At least 5 years of experience operating this equipment
  • Minimum age: 25 years
